2) Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to provide and improve the Service:

  • Account Data: name, email address, password hash (via Firebase Authentication), and user ID.
  • Profile & Wellness Data: skin type, skin concerns, allergies, routine preferences, and saved products.
  • Usage & Analytics: in-app actions (e.g., product tracking, notifications, progress), device and app information (OS, app version), and diagnostic logs.
  • Purchase & Subscription Data: subscription status, product identifiers, and transaction metadata processed by RevenueCat and Apple.
  • Notifications: your preferences and schedules for reminders.

Sensitive personal data: while MySkin may allow you to store information related to your skin health and allergies, we process it solely to deliver the features you request (e.g., recommendations, tracking). Do not upload information you are not comfortable storing.

  • Communicate important updates (e.g., policy changes, service notices).

We do not sell your personal information. We share data with service providers as needed to operate the Service:
